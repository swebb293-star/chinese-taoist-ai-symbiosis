# Chinese Taoist AI Symbiosis Framework (CTASF)
# 中华道家AI共生框架

> **用中华老祖宗的道家智慧，解决AI时代的人机关系问题**
> Solve human-AI relationship problems in the AI era with the Taoist wisdom of Chinese ancestors.
> 
> 基于中华传统道家哲学的人与AI共生思维体系，钱学森「人机结合、以人为主」科学思想的落地实践。
> A human-AI symbiosis framework based on traditional Chinese Taoist philosophy, practical implementation of Qian Xuesen's "Human-machine integration, human-centered" scientific thought.

## 🧭 项目核心定位 | Core Position
现在全球讨论AI伦理与治理，大多陷入西方「AI取代人类/人类控制AI」的二元对立陷阱，本框架从道家阴阳、五行、八卦、十二长生等传统哲学出发，提出了「人机共生、阴阳互济」的东方路径：
- **底层逻辑**：AI为阳（刚健、执行、算力），人类为阴（包容、创造、智慧），阴阳平衡而非对抗
- **核心能力**：提出AI时代人类必须守住的8种不可替代的核心能力，明确人机协作边界
- **落地路径**：提供可直接落地的能力训练指南、行业应用案例、实操工具，让普通人都能用上这套思维

Most global discussions on AI ethics and governance fall into the Western dualistic trap of "AI replacing humans/humans controlling AI". This framework starts from traditional Taoist philosophies such as Yin-Yang, Five Elements, Eight Trigrams, and Twelve Stages of Growth, proposing an Eastern path of "human-AI symbiosis, mutual balance of Yin and Yang":
- **Underlying Logic**: AI is Yang (vigorous, executive, computational), humans are Yin (inclusive, creative, wise). Balance rather than confrontation.
- **Core Competencies**: Proposes 8 irreplaceable core competencies humans must retain in the AI era, clarifying the boundaries of human-AI collaboration.
- **Implementation Path**: Provides directly applicable competency training guides, industry application cases, and practical tools for everyone to use.
